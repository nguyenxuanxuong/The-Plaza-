The Plaza New York

Experience New York's Iconic Luxury Hotel on Central Park South

Since its debut in 1907, The Plaza Hotel has remained a New York icon. Situated on Fifth Avenue, its prestigious address continues to define elegance with unmatched service and an ever-evolving modern sensibility. Experience the magic this spring.

From feast to fete, The Plaza’s extraordinary portfolio of refined experiences is enjoyed by all who pass through the iconic revolving door. Revel in the magic of Afternoon Tea in The Palm Court, savor the sights in the elegant Champagne Bar, and discover our luxurious guestrooms and suites.

Unrivaled luxury and comfort are complemented by attentive and discreet service throughout all areas of the hotel. Explore world-class shopping, indulge at Guerlain Spa, or maintain your routine at The Plaza Hotel Fitness Center.

The opulent Grand Ballroom and Terrace Room, meticulously restored, continue to host many of New York City’s most memorable moments, which are at the heart of The Plaza’s legacy.
